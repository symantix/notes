If, for the sake of argument, DEI is an ineffective hiring process, and if black pilots are being hired based on DEI, then I have questions about their qualifications as well.  If Charlie is wrong about DEI, then that makes him wrong about DEI, and that’s it.  It doesn’t make him racist.  Even if he says, “They’re stealing white peoples’ jobs!”, that doesn’t make him racist, because DEI was intended to find qualified minorities instead of “white” people, and he doesn’t think it works.
