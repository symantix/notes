The only way to keep the dark out, is to radiate light.  It’s not enough just to be a positive person who is pleasant to meet and talk with.  We must radiate our light openly and actively.  Not in a way that bothers people, or annoys or aggravates them.  Sometimes the things we do, which we think are good, bother those around us, simply because they are miserable people, who don’t smile easily.  We must test the waters around us, and find, through well-reasoned guesses, trial and error, what makes the people around us smile, what lifts their spirits.

The only way to change this, is to take an active and positive role in the lives of the community around you.  Even just one neighbor can make a difference.
