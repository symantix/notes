I start my day.

I work on my own stuff instead of Boxzooka.

I sometimes do this for hours.

I start getting worried and stressed.

I do some work.

I write an email, fluffing what I’ve accomplished, and fill it with thoughts and ideas about what I’m working on and what I should work on next.

It always seems to be enough.

I don’t do this on all days, just some.  After a while, I start getting worried that this pattern won’t hold up forever.  So I spend more time with my head in the code, so that no one can say that no progress is being made.  This is especially easy when I have a clear project with hours of time to sink into.
