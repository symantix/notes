There are two small changes that I made in my household, which made a substant, positive difference.  First, I washed a load of dishes.  Second, I sat in the back seat while on a drive, allowing one child to sit next to me, and the other to sit up front.  From the very first time that I did each, there was an immediate, palpable change in the atmosphere.  I have continued to do these two things regularly, and they continue to be hinge points that inspire many kind gestures from my whole family, to each other, without me ever saying a word.
