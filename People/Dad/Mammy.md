Mammy

It’s been more than once that Mammy has made me regret being open with her.

I one day asked you if you ever suspected me of any of the high crimes that Sean committed.  You assured me you didn’t.

The question that Mammy asked at our public event last night demonstrated to me that, not only has she suspected, but she has obviously dwelled on it, enough even to confront me in the most subtle of public fashions.
