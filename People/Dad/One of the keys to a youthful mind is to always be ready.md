One of the keys to a youthful mind is to always be ready, willing, and proactive about changing and starting over.  We all change on the inside.  We get tired of the same old songs, and we want new ones.  Wiping the slate clean and rediscovering ourselves takes us back to the days when we were discovering ourselves for the first time.  Those days were exciting and vital.  The day we forget that we are changing beings, and when we lay down and force ourselves to find contentment in the mundane and redundant, that is the day that we grow old.
