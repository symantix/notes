Things I was taught, that are wrong.

* Write your print penmanship strokes from the top down.  My handwriting looks much better and more controlled when drawn from bottom up.
* You should finish one project before you start another.  This is not how artists think.  They get bored or frustrated, and need to step away, sometimes for long periods.  The only way for a creative mind to stay productive is to have multiple projects in varying states of completion.
