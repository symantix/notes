Should we make an exerted effort to be diverse, or let it come naturally?

If naturally, then how can we overcome tribalism?

If exerted, then how do you feel about affirmative action?

The black culture is very diverse within itself.  I've met some black folk who abhor deferential treatment of any kind, and prefer to make their way by their own will and effort alone.  And I have met those who feel that the ripples of the past couple of hundred years, still unfairly hold them back from their full potential. I say, after all that our ancestors have put them through, shouldn't we make an exerted effort to insure that our black community has all the same opportunities that we do?
