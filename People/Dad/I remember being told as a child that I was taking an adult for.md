I remember being told as a child that I was taking an adult for granted.  I've never understood what it meant to take people for granted.  Why is it bad for me to assume that my loved ones will always be there for me? 

Then I had kids.  And I took them for granted for 10 years.  And then one night, I had a vision of my children dying, and I wept.  It was just a waking dream, nothing superstitious.  I'm not psychic, thank god.  But I've never been the same since.

Taking people for granted.  What a poison that is.
