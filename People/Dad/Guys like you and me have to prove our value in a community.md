Guys like you and me have to prove our value in a community.  The average person doesn't have to do this, because they blend in.  Their is an average level of intelligence that is tolerable among the masses. When you are smart and opinionated, people don't want you around. Unless you can prove your value. Are you funny?  Can you sing?  Can you fix a roof or cut hair?  The average person can't understand the extreme value of our thoughts.  To them, it's like medicine, perhaps even an injection or surgery. They don't understand it, it's distressing, and so they don't want it.  But if we can demonstrate our value in simpler measure, they will tolerate our mental intrusion from time to time.
