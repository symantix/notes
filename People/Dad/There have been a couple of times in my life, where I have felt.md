There have been a couple of times in my life, where I have felt like Moses.  Like a higher power reached down from above, and gave me an instant pile of knowledge and insight, that I didn’t have before.  Like a soundless voice spoke deep into my mind, and in an instant I knew that there was something big that I had to do.

I’m so glad that I didn’t stand on top of a mountain, and tell all of my relatives that these new thoughts were demands that god had placed not just on me, but on all of them as well.

But that was a different time.  Maybe there was no better way back then, to get millions of people to work together.

But is there a better way today?
