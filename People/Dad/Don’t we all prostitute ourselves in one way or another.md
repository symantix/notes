Don’t we all prostitute ourselves in one way or another?  We all sell ourselves in one way or another, and pretend that we’re happy doing the things that get us the money we need to survive, and we all pretend that we like the people who give us that money, whether we really do or not.  Does the metaphorical ever cross over into the literal?

Prostitution is greatest offense was to shove our own depravity right into our face.  It is the worst kind of mirror, but that is our own fault, not theirs.
