I like staying on top of things.  Sometimes I wish the voice that kept me on top of things was a little louder, or spoke more often, or was a bit more assertive.  However, the biggest problem with that voice, is that it doesn't understand that family, art, comedy, studio...these are all things too, that need to be stayed on top of just as much as any other thing.  Ironically, the voice that understands this the best, is the one that wants to avoid the work that makes the money, and instead play until a stroke of creativity hits me.  These strokes will hit me while I'm working in the realm of the dead, but they don't hit me nearly as often, and they are rarely poignant or inspiring.  They're usually small Ideas.
