I hate perfection.  I hate the whole idea of perfection.  For some people, striving for perfection comes naturally, it’s something they were born to do in whatever field they are in, and they are necessary.  But for everyone else, perfection should never be the standard.  The only standard that I should ever have to live up to are my own next steps for whatever I’m doing or want to do.  This is not perfection, it is simply a journey.  I shouldn’t have to worry about falling, in spite of what perfection whispers in my ear.
