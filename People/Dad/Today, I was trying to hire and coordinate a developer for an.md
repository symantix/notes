Today, I was trying to hire and coordinate a developer for an urgent project, and I had several candidates who were interested.  I was trying to decide how much I should keep them all interested, which would essentially waste some of their time.  Then I had this thought:

*Well, I guess I would rather piss them off, than piss my boss off.*

I immediately caught myself after I thought this.  This is how it begins.  This is how societies get corrupted, when we treat other human beings differently in order to satiate the temper of someone with more power.

That said, this is not black & white, and I do plan to keep these guys interested to some extent, until I find the right person.  But I must remain aware, considerate, and respectful of their time.  At the very least, they must sense it in the way I carry myself.
