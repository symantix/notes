When I was a kid, my mom told me that I needed to learn to be responsible.  I was born an artist.  Artists are not responsible.  I've been trying for more than twenty years to be someone I'm not, all because my mom told me to.

I'm not saying I don't need to take care of my kids or wife.  I'm saying that I need a very flexible schedule.  I'm saying that if I would relax and be myself more, unapologetically and generously, I would be happier.  And if I was happier, taking care of my family would feel more natural, as would spending more time with them.

I should think of my children as friends, as peers.
