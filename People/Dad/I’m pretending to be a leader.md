I’m pretending to be a leader.  You may argue that I’m not pretending, but we are surrounded by a world of make pretend, and my play friends have simply allowed me to take on this role.  We, the people, are all that matters, all that is real.  Because as far as anything else is concerned, it’s not about what is real.  That’s a fool’s game.  There are many more fun games to play, and in this life, that’s what it’s about:  Who is playing the game where the most amount of people can have the most fun?  And whose game will last the longest?
