We all struggle with different competing forces within our minds.  The most difficult ones to overcome begin to strike an equilibrium.  Rage vs. civility, lust vs. loyalty, laziness vs. ambition. These battles can last for years.  But eventually, something breaks in the minds of us all.  Many things break, one way or the other.  This is the forge that tempers our greatest heroes, and cracks the minds of our darkest villains.  And then there's you and me, all the folk in between.
