It may be that souls never actually die, and that when our soul leaves our body, it goes somewhere inside or outside of this universe.  And since we can observe with our naked eye, that the wicked have no tolerance for the righteous, it may be that wicked souls can't enter into the place where righteous souls go, not because they are prevented by some celestial rule, but because it is sheer torment for them.  It may be that wicked souls need a place of their own, far from the righteous, full of darkness, where their immense suffering can be alleviated, at least for a short time, by solitude, blindness, or the comfort of other souls who are just as ignorant.
