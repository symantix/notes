Reincarnation is the most beautiful religious concept, to my mind. 

I think many of us really enjoy movies where the protagonist’s suffering resolves to something beautiful or meaningful.  We hope to see this in our own lives, to know that everything we’ve been through meant something, made a difference, if not to the world, then at least to someone we know, or maybe just to ourselves.  And what a miserable way to die, feeling that your life never had any effect on anyone, never mattered.  Maybe we don’t all feel this way, but I’m sure many of us do.  I know I do.  I’m sure my life will effect many, and that makes me happy, and it makes this whole life feel amazing, even fun.  And I’d like to do it again, but please leave me with at least a trace of the memories I have now.  And for those that fail in this life, maybe they would like another go at it.
