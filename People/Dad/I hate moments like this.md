I hate moments like this.  I'm in complete gridlock.  It's the weekend.  I really need to work.  It's 10pm on Sunday.  My client is going to be upset.  I don't have a good reason not to work, except that it's the weekend, and I hate working on the weekend.  This is when anxiety kicks in, and I feel like my world is going to fall apart.  The anxiety won't go away if I work, because I'll start feeling like I'll never realize my dreams of being a great creator.  And it won’t go away if I play or create, because I’ll start feeling like my stability is being yanked from underneath my feet.
