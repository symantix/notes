I struggle each day to keep my head straight about my own ethics.  I have formulated how I should conduct myself at my company when I haven’t been working as much as I should, i.e. never get defensive, always play it cool.  But then when I think about how much I’ve worked, I’m not even sure I’ve done them wrong.  There have been days when I just can’t get motivated, but there have also been days, both recently, and especially months ago, when I would work 10-16 hours, or more, straight, in a single day.  I also feel that I live in a economic culture that is stacked against me, and that the only way I can fulfill my own dreams, is to find every possible way that exists, to both work on my own projects, and get paid enough to make a living.  And right now, that sometimes involves telling my employer that it will take longer to do a task than it actually will.  I have on occasion, found ways to take a whole day off, and still appear as if I’ve made progress the day after.
