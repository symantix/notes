I think I am making some meaningful progress in my efforts to use my anger, while also controlling it.  I had some concerns with myself when I started unleashing these feelings on my boss, on a regular basis.  But then I realized one key difference between my younger self, and me now.  And that is the fact that, after I’m done letting out these feelings, I have the ability to calm down quickly, and speak to the person with respect and calm, almost as if I wasn’t angry.  I also find that I speak to them respectfully while angry, in that I don’t attack them personally, i.e. I don’t call them names or insult them, although they may find the content of my speech insulting as a matter of subjective interpretation.
