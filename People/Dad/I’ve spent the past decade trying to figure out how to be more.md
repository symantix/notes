I’ve spent the past decade trying to figure out how to be more and more relaxed.  I felt like relaxation was the key to lower stress, to heightened creativity.

But it’s not.  Relaxed is not active.  I want to be active, I want to be activated.  I think this whole time I’ve been confusing “relaxed" with "physically comfortable”.  If you’re too relaxed, you don’t feel like doing anything.  I need to be active and comfortable in order to be creative.  

This must be why my most recent instincts have been to leave the TV off as much as possible.  Consuming other people’s content is relaxing, but lacks direct productivity (though it’s sometimes abundantly productive in an indirect way, i.e. being inspired, getting ideas)
