A subscription brokerage service, like Netflix for all things subscribeable.  But rather than owning the products, we simply provide this system as a service to all content providers.  Magazines and newspapers, videos, music, maybe even food.  We figure out how to measure everything in terms of time units per month, and come up with monthly fees, standard and premium, that profit services based on how much time users spent with their products.  This allows subscribers to access multiple premium news sources across the web, using only their browser, by logging in with our auth service.
