One of the greatest gifts I have ever received, is the look in my children’s eyes as they become adults.  It’s a look that says I was a good father.  I have rarely witnessed truth in such a pure form, beyond words, perhaps even beyond interpretation.  When you see truth in such pure form, like when Moses witnessed the face of God on the mountain, it changes you in fundamental ways.  You can feel it happening in real time.  You can’t control it or resist it.  It’s the truth that arises from a faithful marriage or a loyal friendship.
