Capitalism is masculine thought,
Socialism is feminine thought.
Capitalism is the hunter,
Socialism is the gatherer.

Our family has touched so many other people’s lives.  We’ve given of our time and our resources many times over.  Much of the donated time comes through my wife, but it also comes through me.  This is what it means for the two to become one flesh.  She cannot say that she gives her time without me, because without me she would have much less time to give.

Socialism is a necessary part of our society, but it cannot exist without capitalism.  And capitalism without socialism is far too brutal for the most vulnerable and sensitive among us.  Socialism without capitalism means death in this savage world, which cannot be tamed without the savage strength of men.  And the cost and sacrifice of that savagery is far too high not to be rewarded, and the very idea of rewards means that some will get more than others.  The end result of this ideal society is one where all are cared for, most are thriving, and some are wealthy and elite.
