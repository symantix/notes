In abstract, actor and puppet are useful terms to talk about social behavior.  The terms may refer to two people, or both to the same person.  While all act, there are those who control, those who are controlled, and those who control themselves.  And while few of us are ever just one, time and tale have taught us all that self-control is required if you’re going to be a main character.

Among those who control themselves, reasons vary, and self-control is not a tool exclusive to the hero.  Some do so in order to create an optimized version of themselves for the sake of their community.  Some do so in order to mask their hatred of the community.  Sorting out who is who is among life’s greatest challenges, because we are all master actors who have been practicing since birth, and the liars have been studying the truth seekers closely, learning to mimic their behavior.  They are their own puppets, and while some are easy to spot, the master puppeteers may never be spotted.  Our only tools against such deception is that which we offer to our dearest friends:  patience, respect, and the gift of our attention.  Offer these things to all, seek the truth, and most of the puppets will slowly fall away from your life on their own, until all that remain are either your dearest friends, or your worst enemies.  

That’s when some of your most interesting stories will begin.
