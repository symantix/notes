I don’t know what’s going to happen to all of these memories when I’m gone, but I do hope that the universe finds a way to preserve them, every last second.

Sometimes people sound stupid simply because they’re looking at an issue from a completely different perspective.  And as long as we fail to understand that, we will misunderstand them.

One thing is clear to me in this age:  Not one person alive today stands for anything more than part of what is right.  Outside of that, is only destruction.  This is the most important reason to love and listen to people we disagree with.
