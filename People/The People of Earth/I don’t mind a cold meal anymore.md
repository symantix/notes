I don’t mind a cold meal anymore

It used to annoy me intensely, waiting for my kids to prepare their meal, after I had already prepared mine, and was now waiting to watch a show before I began my meal, because the only reason I’m taking time for a show is because there is a meal.  Sitting their waiting on my children, food getting colder by the second, my blood pressure would rise as my mood plummeted.

Years later, my kids now much older, and the same bullshit continues.  My children still take too long to come to dinner, and too long to get it ready.  My meal is still cold when the show begins, and theirs is still warm because it just came off the stove.  Nothing has changed, and yet everything has changed.  

I don’t mind a cold meal anymore.  I finally realized that what I want most, what I need most, I already have.
