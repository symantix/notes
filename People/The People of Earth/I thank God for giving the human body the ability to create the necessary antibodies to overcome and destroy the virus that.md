I thank God for giving the human body the ability to create the necessary antibodies to overcome and destroy the virus that plagues this world.  I thank the scientists, who are the healing hand of God in this moment, for the vaccines that they have created, which allow people to develop those antibodies without actually having to endure the pain and the risks of the virus itself.  And I thank the people — those who took the risk of the first vaccine injections, those who endured the viral invasion of their bodies, and those who lost their lives to this pandemic.  Your memory will live in our hearts for generations to come.
