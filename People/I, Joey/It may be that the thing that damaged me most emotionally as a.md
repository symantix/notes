It may be that the thing that damaged me most emotionally as a child, was public school.  I was bullied quite a bit in elementary school, and in spite of my fond memories of school, I have several visual memories in my mind of other children being violent toward me, or treating me as less than nothing.  I have searched my soul deeply to find blame for my demons, but when it’s when I think of the strangers I encountered on occasion, in my school, and in my neighborhoods, it just begins to feel more and more obvious.  Church was one of the only social scenes where bullies didn’t exist, where even the most popular of athletes must accept and respect you if they want to get into heaven when they die.
