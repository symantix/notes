Find the people in life who truly understand the dynamics of love, reciprocation, and generosity, and cling to them.  Cling to them, and let everyone else go.  If you don’t, you’ll end up as a bitter, self-serving miser, never truly close to anyone, even to those who claim to be your friends and family.  Cherish the friendship of your true friends, and treat them well.  This universe is infinite.  You’re going to need them.
