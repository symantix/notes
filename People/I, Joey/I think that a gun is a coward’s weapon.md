I think that a gun is a coward’s weapon.  The man who fears the cost of peace more than the cost of war, is a step or two behind in the evolution of humanity.  With close combat weapons, the choice to end a life is not so easy or quick.  If you must use a firearm, you should disable your attacker if possible.  There is no coming back from death, no greater offense than to kill.  Death by human hands agitates this world with each passing body, and the only cure is less of it.
