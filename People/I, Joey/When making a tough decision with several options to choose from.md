When making a tough decision with several options to choose from, consider whether there are any options that you know you won’t regret choosing.  Even if the other options might yield greater rewards, you don’t know what the future holds, but you may be able to see that one option will have no regrets no matter how things work out.  Risk can be a good thing, but if the decision is proving impossible to make, choosing an option you know you won’t regret is often the only way to escape the gridlock of your mind.
