We all have a consciousness, and since we communicate with one another as a species, we also have a combined consciousness.  This is a god, in a sense, and it has a will if its own.  It can be cruel or kind.  And that is why a god is always watching, and why it is important to be kind to that god, and make that god proud.  And that means being kind to everyone, standing up for yourself, and demonstrating the kind of good that you wish to see in the world, in the god that is all around you, and a part of you.
