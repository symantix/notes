I learned long ago not to make vows I didn’t know I could keep.  I learned how frivolous new year resolutions are.  So I’ve never made very many of either.  I’ve simply decided to try to achieve my goals, and if I fail, to try again.  And then at some point, I discovered that there was a point with my efforts, where I became stronger, then stronger still. Then I reached a threshold where I knew I could achieve my goal.  It was at that point, that I made a vow, a resolution, one like I had never made before. One of power, and of assurance.
