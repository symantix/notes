I've gotten stoned many many times in my life. When I was young, after smoking for my first few times, I started getting into this mindset that really freaked me out and made me feel very insecure, especially in crowds.  I couldn't figure out what was going on in my head, much less explain it to anyone.

I still start falling to that place every now and then. But since day one, every time I've gone there, I've spent time observing it, and trying to figure out what was going on. I'm forty now, and I think I'm finally starting to understand what's happening.  When I'm stoned, my voice is super loud in my head. Sometimes it almost seems like the same voice is talking to me and from me simultaneously. It used to drive me nuts, because I didn't even understand that that is what was happening, something just felt overwhelming.  But you know we all have more than one voice in our head, mother, father, close friends, enemies.  But they're usually so very quiet, that you usually don't even notice they're there.  Now that I've begun to understand that my own voice is super loud, I've also started understanding that all the other voices are louder too.  Still quieter, but now the threshold between all those voices is way narrower.  Suddenly all the impulsive thoughts that you're usually able to suppress with some ease, are now pretty damn close to the surface.
