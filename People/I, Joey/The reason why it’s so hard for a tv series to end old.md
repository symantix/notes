The reason why it’s so hard for a tv series to end old characters and introduce new ones, without completely destroying the ratings, is similar to the reason why we have such a hard time moving on when we lose our dearest loved ones.  The big question is always, so what could possibly be next?  That person was part of the main plot line. Isn’t the story over now?  It takes imagination, and a willingness to accept new people, new important people, to be able to move on, to be able to keep the show going.
