I got a new board, a Bote Wraith.  It’s a higher end racing/touring board, second that I’ve bought in the past couple of weeks, but best by far.  The wind was high today, the waves were rough, and the current was strong out in the bay here in Pensacola.  I wasn’t even sure I was gonna make it out there because the current was against me, pushing me back into the lake.  I finally got out to some shallow water and walked about a quarter mile or so out into the water.  Then I hopped on the board and let the waves take me back to the shore.  They weren’t big waves, but they were strong and fast, and that board surfed them.  I had a blast. 👍
