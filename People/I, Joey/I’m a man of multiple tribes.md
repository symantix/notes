I’m a man of multiple tribes

I’m a man of multiple tribes.  In every tribe, I have a mom.  In the tribe I was born into, it’s Kit.  In Dr. Jones’ tribe, Rhonda is my mom.  In Alfred’s tribe, it’s Marsha.  In Mary’s tribe, well, she is her own tribe, so it’s her.  In Darryl’s tribe, it’s Tina.  In the first tribe I can remember, it was Mamaw.

My Back Porch Comedy family is a Lord of the Flies situation.

I wish I had understood this sooner.  I think I would’ve treated Mary & Rhonda better.  I never treated them poorly, but I think Mary knows that I’m not fond of her.  And Rhonda has always felt some distance, even if it was small, simply because I couldn't reconcile the idea of her being my mom, when my mom was already my mom.
