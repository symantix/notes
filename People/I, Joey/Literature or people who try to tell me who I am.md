Literature or people who try to tell me who I am.  That includes astrology, fortune telling, science regarding the month I’m born in. I don’t know I’d fear is the word.  I’m fine with reading them, but I tend to avoid them for these reasons:

* I think most of all of them lack substantial evidence for their claims to the knowledge of fate and future.
* If I am to be who they say I am, then it must be of my own accord, untainted by the possibility that I have been influenced by the suggestions that are buried in their foresight.
* To me, the group of people from whom I will allow suggestion, guidance, and influence, is like a bonsai tree at the center of my life. And people who don’t know me, don’t belong there.
