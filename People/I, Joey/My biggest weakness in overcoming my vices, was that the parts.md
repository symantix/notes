My biggest weakness in overcoming my vices, was that the parts of my personality were out of order. Some parts, like the working, responsibile part, were given free reign of my life, while others, like the creative, wilder part of me, weren’t given enough time to express themselves in the light of day.  And so they would push themselves to the surface in the darkness. Once these parts were aligned, the all of me began to work together to become a better compete self.
