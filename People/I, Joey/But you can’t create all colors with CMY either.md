But you can’t create all colors with CMY either.  CMY requires mainly black to complete its spectrum.  The traditional RBY requires mainly white.  Printers don’t use CMY because it’s science, they use it because they’re printing on white, so they can already print as lightly as they need, simply by using less ink, then black to go darker.  Artists are not always painting on white, nor are they always interested in science lol, sometimes they just start blending hue & b&w.  Both systems are imperfect, but both have merit, imo. ♥️

Also, cyan is definitely a shade of blue, as surely as Pluto is a planet. 😂
