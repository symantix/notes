I don’t like President Trump.  I appreciate a couple of things he’s done.  And though I prefer to offer a hand of friendship to everyone I meet, I don’t think that he and I could never be buddies.  

I wish that someone with greater moral fiber was sitting in the Oval Office right now.  But it’s not like I can look back and point to any great examples.  Every president that I have become familiar with has either committed great and terrible sins, or they have been marred by the lies that our people have told about them.  Rarely if ever do I know which it is.  

And so I admire many presidents for some of the things that they have done or decisions that they have made, but I’m not sure that we could ever be buddies.
