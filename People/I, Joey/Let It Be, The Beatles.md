I must admit that my own prejudice kept me from understanding that song for the longest time.  I assumed it was taking about the Virgin Mary, and there was no hatred, but for some reason my mind just tuned out the rest of the song, though I’ve always loved melody.  Turns out Paul is taking about his own mother, and her wise words of peace.  Beautiful.  The message is peace, and that often a mother can bring this to the heart of a child with just a word.
