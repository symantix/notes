Backing out of a driveway or parking spot, and crushing a child who was in a blind spot.  I’ve heard so many stories throughout my life, of children or animals being crushed under a car, usually dying immediately  or moments after.  It haunts me pretty much every time I put my car in reverse.  So I creep slowly back most of the time, just waiting to hearing a sound.  Hoping it’s a bonk, and not a crunch.  If it’s a bonk, maybe I’ll get out of my car and discover the child lying on the ground, bruised but alive, traumatized but alive to learn from their mistakes.
