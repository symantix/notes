From time to time, I dream that I can fly.  Sometimes I’m soaring over the mountains.  Sometimes I just begin to hover off of the ground, and then I just rise up and anywhere I want to go, like I’m in Minecraft creative mode.  It’s all controlled and self-willed, and the ability to fly has come from within me.  In my dream I have discovered flight for myself, I understand and grasp it so well, so thoroughly, that I often become aware even that I’m dreaming, and that it doesn’t matter, because it’s still true.  And for a few moments I lay there, confident that if I just relax and focus, I can will this knowledge from the dream world, into this one.
