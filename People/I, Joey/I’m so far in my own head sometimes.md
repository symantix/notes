I’m so far in my own head sometimes.  Monica will be talking to me, and I’ll start looking at her, but I have no idea what she’s saying, and I don’t even realize that I’m not paying attention to her, or that she’s even speaking to me.  I’ll sometimes be sitting by myself, and my son will walk up and start talking to me, and I become irritated.  In both cases, I try very hard to shift gears once I realize what’s happening, but it’s not easy.  My thoughts in these moments feel very important to me, and I don’t like losing them.
