If my mom died, I would be terribly sad.  I would weep deeply and painfully, many times in the days following it.  I would get through each day, a day at a time, and I would remember her, and it would pain me, as it always has, the great separation that was created between us by some of the choices we both made in my late teens.

If my dad, wife, or daughter died, it would devastate me.  It would feel impossible to even reach a place where I could get through each day, a day at a time.  I would spend a week or more, sitting at the edge of the ocean, unable to do anything but hurt.

If my son died, I might never come back.
