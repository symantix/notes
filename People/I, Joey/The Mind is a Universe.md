If the mind is a universe, then perhaps its memories, experiences, and education are planets, its thoughts are rooms, and its emotions are the weather.  When viewing the mind in this way, it’s easy to understand that regrettable thoughts are not necessarily a poor reflection upon who we are as a person, except that it simply reveals to us the nature of our mind, that it is a vast universe, and that its content encompasses everything that we have been exposed to and more, and that there are some rooms, islands, planets, that we simply should not journey to.  The physical universe is beautiful, and yet all of the planets we have discovered aside from our own, are toxic to human beings.  Perhaps our mind is similar, and similarly beautiful, and perhaps if we find ourself on a toxic planet, we should consider leaving the atmosphere as soon as possible.
