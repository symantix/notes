I’ve always been intrigued by Google glasses. I think that project is pretty much failed at this point, or just reserved for the elite.  I thought the camera was creepy, but the idea of seeing a screen that is simply floating before me, of having a completely emersive AR interface, sounds very useful. I sometimes like to write on my walks, for example, as I’m doing right now, on my phone. But it’s a very blinding experience.  I have only a vague idea of what’s happening around me. So I have to take it slow.  What would help is if I had a giant screen floating way in front of me, maybe 30-40 yards away.  It stays on the road ahead, no matter where I look, unless I reposition it with a gesture.  If a car is coming, it is on front of the screen.
