It's taken me decades to really understand my mom.  Not suggesting that I'm even close to fully understanding her, only that I now realize how little I understood her when I was younger, and how little she has ever understood me.  I've realized that, although I never thought she was perfect, I looked up to her so much when I was a child, that I missed some major flaws. For the longest time, I subconsciously tried to reconcile any crazy thing that she did, with the image that I had of her as a child.
