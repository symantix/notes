Marital Advice

- "I love you" can never be said enough times, in enough ways.
- Let silly voices be commonplace, let jokes abound, and let laughter thrive every day.
- There will be battles.  Choose wisely when to fight for yourself, when to fight for your partner, and when to surrender.  If you want to win the few battles that will be the most important to you, then let your partner win all of the other battles, and let generosity abound between you.
