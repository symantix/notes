Think of your day like a video game.  Like a runner.  You have a limited amount of time to grab as many coins as possible.  As you get better at the game, you learn to work harder, focus sharper.  Soon there are more coins available to grab.  But you’ll never be a true master of the game until you learn to sculpt a daily routine, one that considers not only the coins, but your own well being and happiness, and of those closest to you, and far away.  As your interest for coins becomes balanced with the needs of the world that provides those coins, the availability of coins will become greater still, and you will become a coin magnet.  And as the spirit of generosity grows in the hearts of the people around you, a stronger ecosystem will manifest in the bonds of love, where wealth abounds for all.
