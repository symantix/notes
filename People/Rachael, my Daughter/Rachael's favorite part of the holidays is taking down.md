Rachael's favorite part of the holidays is taking down decorations.  This is especially interesting when it comes to the Halloween decorations, because she takes care of those mostly by herself.  She likes putting them up, but it's lots of work.  She likes having them up, but she has to remember stuff like turning them on and off.  What she really loves about the whole process, apparently the main reason that she even bothers with the whole ceremony of it all, is taking the decorations down after it's all over.
