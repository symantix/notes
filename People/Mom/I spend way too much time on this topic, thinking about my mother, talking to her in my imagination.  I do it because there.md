I spend way too much time on this topic, thinking about my mother, talking to her in my imagination.  I do it because there are so many intelligent thoughts I have about that thread of my life.  But they are thoughts that I will never be able to share with the subject, because she has put up too many walls.  It’s called fruitless effort, I’m dragging dead weight, watering a dead tree, to put it bluntly.  And it’s holding me back.  I should be spending that time thinking about Monica, or my children, or Lincoln Network, or NCG Studios.  There are so many more topics that my imagination can explore in this time, topics that would produce exponentially more fruit in my life.
