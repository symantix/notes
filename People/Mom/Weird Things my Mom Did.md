She once took me on a vacation to a beach somewhere.  We met a lady there, whom she claimed was her friend, though I sensed something strange.  I now recognize what I sense as a lack of genuine familiarity.  My mom told me that we were all staying together in two rooms, and that Inwould probably be sharing a room with this lady, and might even take a bath with her.  As a boy in my early teens, I expressed no objection, and certainly appeared to be on board with this plan.  Then, without explanation, the woman disappeared.  I never shared a room nor a bath.  And I don’t recall ever asking why.
