I don’t like procrastination, but I also don’t like stacking myself up with business.  If I want to veg out right now, do I really have to do something that I thought of or remembered, just because it happened to come to mind?  I can think of a thousand things to do, and keep myself busy all day.  But it doesn’t necessarily follow that the busier you are, the more fulfilled you are.  Be productive, but don’t forget to stop and enjoy a moment, to be with friends, to look at the bigger picture, or maybe just laugh.
