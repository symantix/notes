With regard to decisions that could change your life in substantial ways — job opportunities, moving out or away, alcohol or medication consumption, commitments, sex, marriage, child bearing - always speak to your elders, close friends, and people you respect.  Seek their advice, multiple people that you respect, and try to obtain their blessing.  That’s what I do, that’s what all the adult members of this community do.  These people are the voice of God, the loudest that you will ever hear it.  They will give you open, honest, and specific guidance.  And ultimately, you don’t have to accept any advice except your own.  But if you don’t at least seek, and don’t at least listen, you’re like a lone voice in an echo chamber, ever biased by its own opinion and ideas, an unnourished tree with no branches, a mind without neurons.
