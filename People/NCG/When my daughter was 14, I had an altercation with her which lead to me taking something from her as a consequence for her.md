When my daughter was 14, I had an altercation with her which lead to me taking something from her as a consequence for her actions.  In response, she removed all of the cushions from our outdoor couch, and threw them on the other side of the porch.  At the time, I found her behavior to be disrespectful, and it caused me a great deal of stress.  In hindsight, however, I now realize that the response was also measured.
