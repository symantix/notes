If you’ve had nothing but stress for months and months, it’s time to get creative. Your life isn’t going anywhere, and all meaning is beginning to fade.  If you don’t change your circumstances soon, your life will end prematurely.

If you’ve had nothing but stress for weeks and weeks, it’s time to reflect deeply on your life.  You might be going in circles, just surviving in a dead end situation.  Don’t let it continue much longer.  Find a way to add some positive experiences to your daily or weekly routine.

If you’ve had nothing but stress for days and days, hang on.  Remember why you’re walking through the fire, and if it still seems worth it, keep going.

If you’ve had nothing but stress for hours and hours, this is normal.  Endure the stress, achieve your goals, and relish your free time when it comes.
