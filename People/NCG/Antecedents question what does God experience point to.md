When it comes to antecedents, everything exists, everything is real.  When it comes to antecedents, we are not inquiring into fiction or nonfiction.  Instead, we are only asking what is referenced.  What are we pointing to when we speak, or when we point?  And so when it comes to antecedents, if someone claims to have had an experience with God, the existence of God is no longer in question.  The only question that remains is:  To what does the experience point?  What is God?
