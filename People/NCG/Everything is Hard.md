Everything is Hard
It has always been hard
From the time you were a baby, leaning to crawl, walk, and talk
A child learning to read, write, and add the world up into a tidy sum
A young citizen trying to find your place in society, learning to survive
Or a fully grown human who now understands that we must do more than just survive, a fully formed person who is looking back on all that we have lost along this journey, all that was worth keeping, finding again, learning anew, and discovering for the first time.
Life is hard, and it will always get harder, until we stop reaching to grow further.  And childhood only seems easy looking back, and what was difficult then, now seems natural.
