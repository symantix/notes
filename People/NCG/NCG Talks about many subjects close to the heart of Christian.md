NCG Talks about many subjects close to the heart of Christian people.  Sometimes, we talk about the Bible, and we cherish the stories and the heroes, and we wrestle over their morals and meaning.  But we don't require anyone to swear allegiance to Jesus.  No true hero requires allegiance from anyone.  The Bible itself asks no one for allegiance, except by example.  The truth is, heroism fosters allegiance on its own.
