If you confess your sins, God will be faithful and just to deliver you.  You have to start by being honest about your problems.  Being honest with yourself is a good start, but if a problem continues, the power and impact that comes from confessing to others, can’t be underestimated.  Did you know that there are some problems that are so sensitive, that we can only confess them to our mothers and fathers, our husbands and our wives, or someone who is closest to you?  And that’s okay.  But remember that only the simplest of problems can be resolved alone.  For everything else, find the people that are closest to you, at least one, and seek insight in their counsel by telling them the truth.
