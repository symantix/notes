My dad has taught on giving around 5 times in the entire time I’ve known him, and I’ve discussed it with him a few more times privately.  His philosophy always resonated with me.  Long before I knew him, I used to pray, “God, help me to become wealthy, so that I can show you what I can do with it.”  Since then, this feeling began to grow in me.  In my earlier life, I would say, giving keeps me close to God.  These days, I continue to believe that it keeps me well-grounded in the world around me.  In keeps me connected to my family, my community, and to the Earth.  It reminds me that, yes, we were all born to fly, we were all meant to soar above the mountain tops; but we were also meant to come back to the Earth, to our home, and to build there, among our people.  And so I have chosen where to build, and where to give, and I have chosen to continue building and giving until the day I die.
