Welcome to Notebooks

Notebooks is the only notebook you ever need. It allows you to create as many books as you need to write, store and structure your details of life, and it will quickly become the one and only repository for all journals, ideas, notes, drafts and diaries but also for your projects, task lists, documents, files and everything else you want to keep close at hand.

Notebooks stores and displays almost any type of document: plain text and formatted text, PDF, web pages, MS Office or iWork documents, photos, videos, music and much more. Everything you would usually print out and file can now go directly into Notebooks, without adding bulk.

But Notebooks is not only about documents and files. You can also keep your task lists in Notebooks and tick off your to-dos as you complete them. Divide your projects into nested sub projects, which makes them easier to handle, and cross-reference into your reference library, which, of course, is in Notebooks, too.
When a task reaches its due date, Notebooks' badge on the home screen reminds you of pending business, and an optional acoustic alert tries to draw your attention.

Notebooks – A Combination of Multiple Apps

You would usually need a collection of multiple apps to cover the whole functionality of Notebooks, but combining all that in a single app not only avoids cluttering your iPad or iPhone with countless apps, it also makes your workflow more efficient, and it even saves you money. - Notebooks combines the following apps:

  • Note Taker
  • Word Processor
  • Task Manager
  • File Storage
  • Markdown Editor
  • HTML to Markdown Converter (Markdownify)
  • Clipboard Manager
  • PDF Converter
  • eBook Creator

Usage Scenarios

There are countless opportunities to use Notebooks. The list below is just a random selection of situations in which you can take advantage of Notebooks.

  • Capture your notes and ideas where ever you are.
  • Work on an article, a blog entry, your research paper, diary or travel journal while on the go.
  • Manage your writing project and sync it with Scrivener or with Notebooks for Mac and PC 1.
  • Sync the contents of Notebooks on your iPhone with Notebooks on your iPad.
  • Type meeting minutes, split them into tasks and delegate the tasks while on the way back to the office.
  • Keep and manage your task lists, tick off tasks as you complete them and let Notebooks alert you of due dates.
  • Store guidelines, handbooks and any kind of information you need to reference quickly.
  • Grab snippets from Safari, Mail or any other app and store them in Notebooks.
  • Import, open and save documents from other apps on your iPhone or iPad.
  • With a few taps, turn your writings into an eBook and open it in iBooks or share it with others.

Feature Highlights

Notebooks provides a huge number of functions, features and details. It is almost impossible to list all of them, so we highlight the most important ones below. - Each new release augments the capabilities of Notebooks.

Create and Process Documents

  • Create Formatted documents (HTML) and use WYSIWYG editing to produce beautifully formatted texts with font styles, colors, lists, embedded images etc.
  • Assign document styles (CSS) to your formatted documents.
  • Create Plain Text notes if you do not want to care about styles and format. You can always convert them to a formatted document later.
  • Use Markdown tags in plain text documents and convert them to formatted document later. You can also tell Notebooks to convert those Markdown documents on the fly and assign a custom document style.
  • Type your notes and texts, no matter how long they are. If you want, Notebooks counts words or characters for you.
  • Define your own sets of extra keyboard keys to quickly and easily access special characters.
  • Use landscape and full-screen modes to maximize screen space and enjoy distraction-free reading and writing.
  • Freely assign fonts, colors and backdrops (paper types) to your notes. Notebooks provides a convenient set of colors and paper types, but you are free to define and add your own.
  • Convert your documents to PDF. Any document that Notebooks is able to display can be converted to PDF.
  • Turn single documents or whole hierarchies of books into an eBook to read in iBooks or share with others.
  • Compile multiple documents or the contents of a book into one single, formatted document.

Import Documents

  • Import text, HTML, RTF, PDF, MS Office, Apple iWork documents, pictures, websites, web archives, audio files and videos.
  • Import iPhone/iPad Notes and Palm Memos.
  • Quickly create new documents from the contents of the Pasteboard (text, images, URLs, websites,…). This makes Notebooks your Clipboard Manager.
  • Grab web pages directly from Mobile Safari (you need the Notebooks Bookmarklet for that, it is available in the download area of our website).
  • Use “Open in” to send documents from other apps to Notebooks.

Voice Memos

  • Use Notebooks as a voice recorder; recording takes place in the background, so you can continue to work on documents and type you written notes at the same time.
  • Copy a memo’s link, insert it into a formatted document and you have an embedded audio note.

Sketches

  • Notebooks can be your sketchbook and allows you to draw or write with your fingers or an optional stylus.
  • You can create a sketch from scratch, or you open an image to paint on.
  • Rotate the sketch (or image).
  • Use the current paper pattern as background for your sketch.

Task Management

  • Create, organize and manage your Task Lists.
  • Create nested task lists if you want.
  • Assign due dates and alarm times to tasks.
  • Assign recurring due dates to your tasks if you want.
  • Let Notebooks extract tasks from your documents automatically.
  • Maintain your reference library of documents that support you with your task management.
  • Define contexts and assign them to books, tasks and documents. This helps you find information by context.
  • If you wish, Notebooks can automatically extract the context tags from your documents, too.
  • Split your notes to tasks, or let Notebooks extract tasks from your notes.

Organization and Structure

  • Store and organize notes in nested books.
  • Freely move, rename and restructure your notes and books.
  • Sort notes by title, modification, creation date or your custom sort order.
  • Find your notes with full text search (plain text, HTML, iWork, Office, RTF and web archives).
  • Protect confidential notes with a passcode.
  • Use Notebooks’ custom links (URLs) to reference notes or books within Notebooks and across applications.

Convenient Gestures

  • Swipe left or right across a document to move to the next or previous document in the book.
  • Swipe left or right while editing to hide the keyboard.
  • Tap a document with two fingers to switch to full-screen mode. Tap again to return to normal view.
  • Tap a PDF document with two fingers for a more convenient view with thumbnails.
  • Pinch or zoom to change the font size of plain text documents.

Synchronization

  • Synchronize Notebooks with Dropbox and your computer in one swoop. This allows you to sync Notebooks across multiple devices like iPhone, iPad, and Notebooks on your Mac or PC.
  • Synchronize Notebooks with WebDAV servers .
  • Access Notebooks directly from your computer through its built-in WebDAV server.
  • Exchange documents with your computer through iTunes’ file sharing.
  • Send notes and documents by Mail or print them out.
  • Use the “Share” option to send documents to Facebook or Twitter.

Integration with other apps

  • Use the “Open in” menu to send documents to other apps.
  • Type faster and easier by integrating with TextExpander (if you have that installed).
  • Send tasks to OmniFocus, Things, Firetask, Ideabook or Appigo Todo (the applications need to be installed)

Enjoy carrying your world in your pocket!
