My Wife & I Don’t Fight

I don’t know why.  It’s been suggested here that this may be a sign of a disfunctional marriage.  You shouldn’t fight too much, definitely no yelling or violence, but total peace?  Well, you know you want to believe in something like that, you want to enjoy it and have faith that’s it’s true.  But it could be like, the calm before the storm, you know?  Maybe we’ve been in the eye of the hurricane for 17 years.

I don’t think that’s the case.  Though I’ve recently learned that this peace might be mostly because of my wife.  You know, like I’m the asshole and she’s just not speaking her mind.  That’s okay, if that’s the storm thats , then I happily await it’s arrival

- We broke up over this.  I believe in equality in marriage.
- I don’t want a wife who doesn’t speak her mind
- We discuss a lot of things.  We don’t always agree.  But we almost never become angry
- We definitely get a little annoyed with each other sometimes.  But that is usually a passing moment
- Our marriage isn’t perfect
    - Monica suffers from being a Mom.  Sometimes she loses her mind at the house.
        - Definitely speaks her mind to the children
    - I’m a pervert

        - My gifts in the arts can be a burden.  If I don’t follow through with my ideas and waves of inspiration, I will never increase in skill, experience, speed.  So I must dedicate myself to my craft daily, and keep my ideas organized so that I know what to work on.
        - My career is a huge pain in the ass.  Doing what other people want, instead of what I want?  Being in a particular spot during a certain time of day, every day, for 40+ years?
