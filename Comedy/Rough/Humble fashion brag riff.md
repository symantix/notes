I am a strong and talented person.  In fashion, however, I aim to dress simply and conservatively, with just a touch of uniqueness.  I like to say, I can stand out if that’s what’s needed from me, but I don’t want to be separate from my friends, neighbors, and colleagues.  I don’t want to dress so loudly that I drown out everyone around me, because part of my strength as a human being comes from being humble.  I am a very humble person.  Just ask anyone who knows me, and they will tell you just how much I brag about being humble.
