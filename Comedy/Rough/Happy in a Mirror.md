When I look in the mirror and smile, I’m reminded of what I look like when I’m happy.  It’s a nice picture, so yes, I feel happier.  I’m also reminded that I don’t really need a reason to feel happy.  I just need all of the thoughts that make me sad, to go away for a while.  That’s all that anybody really needs.  When we begin to feel like we are a bad person for stepping away from our heavy thoughts for a while, when we feel that we are somehow obligated to carry them without rest, that is when we lose our ability to be happy at will.
