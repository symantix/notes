Family of the Flies

It’s nice to feel at home in a new tribe.

It occurred to me recently that I’m a man of multiple tribes.  And in every tribe, I have a mom and dad.  In the tribe I was born into, Kit was mom, and my dad was murdered by a telephone pole, long story.  So Kit was also my mother-fucking-dad.  Thank god for badass women, right?  In my wife's tribe, it’s Marsha & Alfred.  In my studio tribe, it’s Rhonda & Dr. Jones.

And now here at Back Porch Comedy… you know it’s more of a Lord of the Flies situation.

Where all of you are Jack Merridew

and I’m basically just Piggy

I mean, I know you love me, but I’m just one wrong opinion from being bashed over the head with a rock.

It’s still special though.
