Getting Out of Jury Duty

TRY SLOWING DOWN THE PUNCHLINE.  Let them laugh, then say the second line. 

I keep getting called into jury duty. No matter how many excuses I turn in, they call me back in the next month.  I finally went in.  The defense lawyer asked me if I had ever used any illegal drugs. He didn't ask anybody else that question.  And I think the defendant was being tried for drug use. I was about to be roped into jury duty guys. I had to think on my toes.  I'm no good at that guys.  So I just shot straight with guy. I said...

BRING THE PERPETRATOR BEFORE ME THIS INSTANT!  I WOULD SCRUTINIZE HIS SOUL!

I was dismissed immediately.
