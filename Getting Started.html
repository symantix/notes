# Getting Started We have tried to make Notebooks as simple and intuitive as possible, but because of the considerable number of options we have prepared handbooks and guides: - **[Quickstart Guide][]** - **[Handbook as PDF][]** - **[Handbook as eBook][]** These documents will help you get started with Notebooks in no time: - Tap any of the links above and Notebooks will start downloading the document in the background (you can tell by the spinning indicator in the status bar). - A few moments later you will find the document at the top level of Notebooks. You can open and read the PDFs in Notebooks, but for reading the eBook, please choose your favorite eBook reader. For more documentation, please also take a look at: - The [Notebooks website](https://www.notebooksapp.com) - Our [Blog](https://www.notebooksapp.com/news) - Our [FAQ](https://www.notebooksapp.com/faq) Should you have further questions, you are welcome to contact us by [email](mailto:info@notebooksapp.com).
