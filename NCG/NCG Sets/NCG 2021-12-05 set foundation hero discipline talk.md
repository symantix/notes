    - In most good stories, there is always a hero.  And that is true of life.
        - Heroes are the ones who are prepared, 
        - through education and discipline, 
        - to sharpen their abilities 
        - To survive moments of crisis
        - To lead and protect others
        - To help the helpless
        
 And so, through storytelling we shows ourselves visions of the past and future, and we create the image of the ideal human in those places, and we lift the story up for everyone to examine and discuss together.  It is the language of theology, but more broadly speaking, it the language of shared thought, and philosophical evolution.  This is one big reason why it’s so important to explore the images of humanity with our own lives, our own, our own education, and our own self discipline.  Discipline requires doing things that you don’t always want to do, every day, in order to build the strength of the body, mind, and spirit.  And education requires the daily discipline of learning how to accomplish the goals that will require our strongest and most prepared selves.
