If you’ve ever bought a pre-made, frozen chili cheese dog, you’ve probably looked at it and noticed that, yeah, it really is designed, in every way, for a person that doesn’t care about their life.  It’s like they’ve profiled that person in some use-case scenario, maybe a focus group.  And there’s a post-it note written with big bold red marker, pinned to a board on the wall, HEALTH IS IRRELEVANT.  You can have your pre-made dollar chili cheese dog.  You don’t have to fry or grill any franks, or warm up any chili, or melt any cheese.  It’s just sitting there, waiting for you.  It can sit there for months.  You want it?  You can have it in 60 seconds.  All that tough spongy bread, that shriveled up frank, and convenient store pump chili, and pump cheese.  It’s disgusting, anti-health, and that’s why you love it.  I had one once.  I might get another, one day.
