2. Filming without plan is a waste
*learn about storyboards and shot lists. Storyboards visual outline of the movie. Shot lists are exactly what it sounds like: a list of each shot you need to finish your storyboard.

- Guess what a storyboard is
	- Used to excite the imagination and visualize the final edit
- Shot list
	- An outline of shots based on the storyboard

3. Film. Then film some more
*experiment with different shots, angles and camera options. Don't worry about the final product in this step-that comes later.

4. Action!
*Now it's time to put everything together, pack up and start filming. With your fellow Cadettes to turn your hard work into a 5 minute movie.

5. Edit and premiere your movie!
*Pulling everything together for your movie can be simple as clicking and dragging film clips with a basic digital editing program. From there
