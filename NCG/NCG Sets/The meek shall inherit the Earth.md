The meek shall inherit the Earth.  The meek and the kind, and the humble.  The honest and the considerate.  The honorable, the empathetic.  The loving and compassionate.  It may take a few more millennia, maybe even more, who knows.  But that is where our species is headed, a kinder gentler species.  Earth is our teacher, our journey, our destination, and our inheritance.  We settle the Earth by settling ourselves.
