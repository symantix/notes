# DAIS — Marketing Video Script
**Audience:** Investors & Funders | **Tone:** Sharp & Intellectual | **Runtime:** ~2 minutes

---

## OPENING — "The Problem"
*[VISUAL: Rapid-fire montage of chaotic social media feeds, comment wars, viral outrage, reply-guy pile-ons. Fast cuts. Discordant audio noise fades in.]*

**VO:**
The internet gave everyone a voice.

*[VISUAL: Hard cut to black. Silence.]*

**VO:**
Then it gave them an algorithm to scream into.

*[VISUAL: Stats flash on screen — "Social media engagement peaks with anger." "73% of users report feeling worse after scrolling." Cut to a lone thoughtful person staring at their phone, then closing it.]*

**VO:**
Somewhere along the way, discourse stopped being a conversation — and became a performance. Designed not for truth, but for time-on-screen.

---

## PIVOT — "The Idea"
*[VISUAL: Clean, quiet. Typography appears on a dark background: the word "DAIS" — a raised platform, a place to be heard.]*

**VO:**
DAIS is built on a different premise.

That the best ideas don't go viral. They go deep.

*[VISUAL: Elegant product UI appears — clean lines, editorial layout, clearly structured conversations.]*

**VO:**
DAIS is an invite-only discussion platform — part magazine, part think tank, part structured council — built for people who have something worth saying, and an audience worth saying it to.

---

## FEATURE WALKTHROUGH — "How It Works"
*[VISUAL: Split-screen panel and assembly discussion layout. Smooth UI animations.]*

**VO:**
Every post has two layers.

The **Panel** — a structured, invite-only exchange between the author and expert voices. No crosstalk. No pile-ons. Just substantive dialogue on the record.

And the **Assembly** — an open but organized space where the broader community can respond, upvote, and anchor comments to specific points in the discussion.

*[VISUAL: Magazine-style curated homepage. Editorial team pinning featured articles.]*

**VO:**
A real editorial team curates the best of it — daily — into a magazine-quality publication. Not a feed. A front page.

*[VISUAL: Community moderation UI — Docket, condemnation flow, Boneyard.]*

**VO:**
Community moderation is transparent and human-driven. No shadow bans. No invisible algorithm. Members judge what belongs — and the record is clear.

*[VISUAL: AI participant with robot avatar clearly labeled. Engaged in discussion thread.]*

**VO:**
And where AI participates? It's marked. Always. Because on DAIS, trust is the product.

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY — "Why Now"
*[VISUAL: Whitespace. Minimal graphics. Feels like a well-designed report.]*

**VO:**
Social media is a $150 billion industry built on fractured attention and manufactured outrage. The backlash is here. Advertisers are nervous. Users are leaving.

What no one has built — until now — is a structured alternative that takes quality seriously from day one.

DAIS isn't trying to out-algorithm anyone.

It's doing something harder. And rarer.

It's building a platform people actually want to come back to.

---

## CLOSE — "The Invitation"
*[VISUAL: The DAIS wordmark. Clean. Confident. Stationary.]*

**VO:**
DAIS is invite-only by design — seeded with journalists, academics, creators, and domain experts across every major field.

Early members get lifetime access to premium features, free.

We're raising now to hire the editorial team, grow the seed community, and prove what structured discourse can look like at scale.

*[VISUAL: Tagline fades in.]*

**VO:**
*"Where speech finds its form."*

*[VISUAL: Contact / website URL. Hold on DAIS wordmark.]*

---

## SCRIPT NOTES

- **Estimated runtime:** 1:50 – 2:05 at a measured, deliberate pace
- **Narration style:** Single authoritative voice — calm, unhurried, confident. No hype.
- **Music:** Minimal, intellectual — think sparse piano or ambient strings. Should support, not overpower.
- **UI footage:** Requires polished product screen recordings or high-fidelity mockups
- **Key visual metaphor:** The contrast between noise (social media chaos) and form (DAIS structure)
