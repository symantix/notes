## These are the virtues that hold me together

- Sleep schedule
- Work ethic 
- Diet
- Exercise
- Sexual purity
- Devotion to family
- Devotion to community
- Restraint with drugs and alcohol

This is devotion to God.

## These are the virtues that can be relaxed (a little) during a holiday

- Sleep schedule, party late, sleep late
- Work ethic, don’t push yourself so hard
- Diet, enjoy some cheesecake or something
- Exercise, do it or don’t, whatever makes you happy
- Devotion to community, alone time is important too
- Restraint with drugs and alcohol, enjoy yourself

However, many of these virtues will lead to a more enjoyable and fulfilling holiday.  It’s important to realize this in your journey toward the ideal.
