The kids encounter an alien starship.  A few aliens are exiting the hatch.  They are all dressed in hazard suits that have a digital display for a face, and on that display is the head of a cow, 8-bit style.

Alien: Moo.
Kid: Uh, hi.  Why do you all have cows on your faces?

The aliens converse briefly.

A: We were seeking the most intelligent life on your planet.  We thought that some familiarity might set them at ease.
K: But…that’s just a cow.

Aliens converse again.

A: Will you take us to Acow please.

Kid shrugs, then takes them all to a pasture.

K: Alien, meet cow.

A moment of silence.

A: Moo.
Cow: Moo.
