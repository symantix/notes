The Bible tells us that everything is from God, in God, and through God.  

The question of infinite recursion proposes a maddening thought exercise.  It’s a question that can’t be answered.  No matter how much information we gather from the universe, we will never be able to answer this question.  It simply points out how vast time truly is.

And yet, there is one possible answer, to a question that has no answer.  And that is, that perhaps we’re asking the wrong question.  The question that we usually end up discussing is, where do we come from?  But that is not the question we’re answering when we grapple with infinite recursion, because infinite recursion deals with time, not place, and it ultimately teaches us that we can never satisfy the answer of time.  When is not a question with a complete answer, or even a fragment.  Where is a simpler question.  Where did we come from?   We came from the Land.  We were born from the Earth.  And that is the only sure answer that any of us will ever get.
