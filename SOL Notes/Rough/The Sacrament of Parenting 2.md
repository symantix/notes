This thing that we give our children, called parenting, it’s a holy thing.  It’s sacred.  Not to suggest that it is always performed divinely, but as measured by the depth to which we regret our mistakes.  Oh you think you’ll go to hell if you don’t accept Jesus as your personal lord and savior?  Try yelling at your children for the rest of their youthful lives, try riding their ass about every little mistake they make, and then watch them slowly sail away as adults from the miserable childhood that you provided for them, never to spend another quality moment by your side except on the occasional holiday, and then ask yourself what hell really is, and what gospel we should actually be spreading.
