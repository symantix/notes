- Anger boils.  Getting mean.  Climaxes with disaster.  Some in Kikay’s team go overboard with a prank, and destroy T&T’s ship.  Fire.  It’s devastating.  Maybe T&T catch them in the act.  A diverse group of kids, 3-5 of them.  They taunt T&T as they run, saying some things that imply their loyalty to Kikay.  At times they look like monsters, or ancient tribal masks, dancing around their burning dreams.  We end this issue with T&T standing in front of the bonfire that is their ship.  Full page, except for bottom (or top? Yeah top) horizontal sliver panel, showing kids’ eyes, in tears, Tiq’s eyes also bear obvious rage.
- T&T plot their revenge.  Tiber is really upset, but isn’t convinced that revenge is the solution to their problem.  Their problem is that they don’t have a ship.  The only way to solve that problem is to start over.  But they can’t do that when they’re living near such a bully.  Tiq gets mad and leaves, declaring she is done.  A page or two without her.  Then a page, much later in the day, with her venting on a junk yard cliff. We can’t tell if it’s quietly or in her head.  Poetic and ultimately a surrender to calmer thinking. Her later return is no surprise to Tiber.
    - Or maybe she winds up on the cliff after a journey, screaming at the air, collapsing to the ground.  
    - Prior to that, she’s walking along the sidewalk.  A car stops and the door opens. Tiq recognizes the person.  They drive to the hospital and see her sick mother.
    - Later, she’s walking to the junk yard.  She’s mean to a person or two along the way.  A car that isn’t looking where it’s going.  A squirrel exists in her proximity.
