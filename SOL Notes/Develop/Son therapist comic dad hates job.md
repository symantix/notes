DAD is laying down on the couch with SON sitting on a chair next to the couch as if they are in a psychiatrist's office.  The Son holds a comic book as if it is a notebook.  The Dad is obviously very tired, rubbing his eyes and generally looking like hell.

1.
Son:  You seem stressed-out lately.
Dad:  I'm just trying to take a nap...

2.
Dad:  I keep having NIGHTMARES about work.
Son:  Sounds serious.  Maybe subconsciously you hate your job.

3.
Son:  How DO you feel about your job?
Dad:  I hate it.

4.  Son looking at Dad over his glasses.
Son:  Oh, then those aren't nightmares--you're just dreaming.  You're doing fine.
Dad:  Thanks.
