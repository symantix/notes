The Air Car...


...should take a long time to build in this story.  It should be one of the things that people wonder about...is it real, or just kids playing games?  It should test the readers, to see if they're going to believe in the kids or not.  It should be drawn out over time, and become the symbol of hope, determination, imagination...and then, finally, one day, get built.  Maybe even built, but then it doesn't work, tested, and it crashes.  But never given up on, and one day it does work.
