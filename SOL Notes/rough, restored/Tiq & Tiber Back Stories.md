Tiq & Tiber Back Stories

Tiber finds out that he comes from a long line of viking ship builders.  We have a short arc where his dad has long Viking hair beard horned helmet, the works, like your 6-dollar tee.  It’s still in space though, maybe Red Rising style (remember that Viking dude in space?)

The backstory I think is too much, but a make-believe story where his dad has joined them on a Viking adventure.  “Son, you’ve gotta quit pulling me into your pretend adventures.  I have too much work to do”. Tiber, “What is this that you speak of, Dadnar Nomane” Dad “is that a bald joke?”  Tiq “You squawk like a girl!  TO BATTLE!”

Tiq gets in some sort of accident, and ends up in the hospital for a while.  While unconscious, she encounters her (now dead) mother, and has an adventure with her that culminates with her mother facing death with her, imparting her experience and wisdom, and sending Tiq back to the land of the living.
