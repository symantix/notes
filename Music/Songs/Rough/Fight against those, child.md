From this letter to my children:

Unfairness flows like water in this world, child.  So abundantly.

You must either learn to accept it, and find happiness in spite of it, or you must learn to fight it. Probably somewhere in between.

But if you choose to fight, my child, I encourage you not to fight against any of the minor unfairnesses that are brought upon you by those who love you most.

Wait until you're an adult.  

Wait until you've looked the true unfairnesses of this world in the eye, and witnessed the crushing pressure of the societal expectations that surround you.

Fight against those, child.  Fight against those.
