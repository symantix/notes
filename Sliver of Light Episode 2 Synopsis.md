Sliver of Light, Episode 2 continues our journey beyond the garage, where the consequences for space exploration can be terribly unterrestrial.  Dreams bleed into daylight, confidence becomes camouflage, and the nascent unearth a primitive artifice.

This issue unfolds across richly illustrated, full-color pages packed with visual storytelling, layered environments, and comic beats that reward careful reading.  Industrial sprawl collides with humble beginnings, and ancient machinery speaks of impossible realities.

Written and illustrated by Joey Livingston, Episode 2 deepens Sliver of Light’s sense of scale as the story moves outward and begins colliding with a world already in motion.
