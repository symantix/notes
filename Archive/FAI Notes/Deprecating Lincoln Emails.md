Deprecating Lincoln Emails

@channel By the end of the year, we all need stop accessing our Lincoln emails directly.  Here’s what that means right now:

- All 3rd party accounts that use your Lincoln email need to be updated to use your FAI email.  
- This includes logins that use Google’s protocol.  There should be a way to switch any such login to use your FAI account.
- If anyone important is still emailing you at your Lincoln account, you need to update all systems and notify all people to email you at your FAI account.

And here’s what this means for next year:
- In January 2025, we will be suspending as many Lincoln accounts as possible.
- If you don’t see any way to close down your Lincoln email completely, you need to request an exception.
- If you are granted an exception, we will setup a forward to your FAI account (if one is not already in place).
- We will change the passwords to all active Lincoln accounts, regardless of exceptions.  Any active accounts will forward as-needed, without being accessed directly.

These steps will help to insure that we continue to transition away from these old accounts completely.  If you need assistance with any of this, or have any questions, please let me know.
