# "Touch-Base" summary

**Date:** Apr 15, 2026  
**Time:** 10:29 AM – 10:35 AM GMT-5:00  

---

## Summary

The company eliminated the CTO position due to strategic pivot, offering severance and support for Joey's transition.

### Key Points

#### CTO Position Elimination

The company eliminated the CTO position due to organizational restructuring.

* The organization eliminated the CTO position, stating it was not an indictment of Joey's work but due to organizational changes and restructuring.
* Joey received a three-month severance package with full benefits.

#### Joey's Questions and Concerns

Joey sought clarification on the decision and expressed his feelings.

* Joey asked if his performance was a factor, to which the company responded, "No."
* Joey felt he lost trust and that his role as CTO became "in name only" after being given significant privilege and responsibility.
* Joey confirmed that he should cease all work immediately, effective right now.

#### Company's Explanation and Responsibility

The company acknowledged responsibility for the role's diminished scope.

* The company admitted fault, stating they pivoted their business model, making product generation less central, and donor appetite for those products was not present.
* The company acknowledged that the nature of the CTO role was not as expansive as originally envisioned.

#### Equipment and Future Support

Joey secured equipment and received offers for future career assistance.

* Joey was allowed to keep the company equipment he possessed.
* The company offered to provide strong recommendations and referrals for Joey's next endeavor, specifically mentioning opportunities for engineers or IRS at GS 15.

#### Professional Farewell

Both parties expressed mutual respect and wished each other well.

* Joey expressed no hard feelings and hoped to stay in touch.
* The company appreciated Joey's professionalism and wished him luck in his next role, considering him a friend.

### Action Items

#### Mark

* Send agreement outlining severance and benefits to Joey.
* Connect Joey with engineering/IRS opportunities.

#### Joey

* Log out of all systems today.
