Citizens’ Voice

A site designed to allow people to express their concerns on any topic surrounding government, I.e. policies, problems, solutions, overreach, corruption, etc.  We use a voting model to allow the community to raise their favorite posts to the top.  The best posts are selected using internal criteria, community popularity being one measurement:

- Community popularity 
- Post length (longer posts are more likely to be more comprehensive)
- We use AI to analyze and identify well-formed thought 
- Historical reputation of authors
- We use AI to identify and avoid posts with ad-hominems or other critical fallacies.
- Other criteria to be determined 

The site is organized by state and county, and the posts that rise to the top get passed to relevant civil servants.

We also use AI to identify potential bad actors within our communities.

Posts are embeddable and shareable.
