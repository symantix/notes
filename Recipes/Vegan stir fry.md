Vegan stir fry

Pan sear in a small amount of avocado oil and 2 tbsp water
Broccoli, florets and sliced stocks 
Carrots, sliced or baby
Diced pablanos
And 1-4 crushed garlic cloves
Arrange in cold pan 
Sprinkle coarse salt
Cover pan
Turn burner close to high
5 minutes
check and push down broccoli florets
Add thickly diced onions 
Cover pan for 5 more minutes

Add to pan:
thickly diced mushrooms, 
A touch more salt,
a few red pepper flakes,
maybe a small amount of spicy chili crisps in oil
Flip broccoli 
5 minutes or more uncovered, looking for tenderness and char
Turn heat to medium and minute or two before done, to prepare for noodles.

Add noodles to boiling water for 3 minutes, drain water completely 

Transfer pan contents to a bowl
Add garlic and herb
Add raw diced tomatoes 

Pan sear noodles in sesame oil,
with soy, 
unagi, 
and Asian noodle seasoning

Combine and enjoy.
