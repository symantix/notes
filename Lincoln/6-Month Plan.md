6-Month Plan

I wake up every morning and think about how I can do a little bit better today than I did yesterday.  I might work on becoming more organized, or learning something I didn't know before, or focussing longer and deeper, or tightening up my schedule, or stepping away from the trees to see the forest.  I have highs and lows in these struggles, but my lows today are higher than my highs from earlier in my career.

I'm not sure how to define or quantify these goals any more measurably, but I'm open to suggestions.  If I were to aim to win an art contest, for example, the only quantifiable measure, at the end of the day, is the judge's opinion.  So when it comes to those goals in life for which subjective quantification is a must (if only in part), I aim to make my achievements extremely clear, and eliminate any doubt with regard to my success.

Goals:

- Continue streamlining my daily routine by competing with myself daily to be more efficient and focussed
- Continue improving skills in Javascript, Node, Vue, and React by studying on a regular basis.  I have a subscription to Packtpub, which has a mountain of books on programming.  The subscription is paid for by Lincoln, and I turn it off and on as my schedule allows time for it. 
- Improve leadership and management skills by making weekly meetings a matter of discipline, and continuing to always make time and be responsive to all team members.  I mainly see this as a muscle that just needs to be worked out daily.
- Build and organize a skilled engineering team for Studio (whatever size our budget allows)
- Clear a full day of my weekly schedule by recruiting talented and hard working engineers that I can trust.  I will use this time as an additional path to accomplish all goals outlined here.
- Improve fitness/health, making sure my body and mind are prepared for daily challenges and stress, both on and off the job.
