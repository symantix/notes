Debate Site

The trouble with modern social media site is that the conversations are not organized.  The best they have to offer is groups, but the conversations lack organization there as well.

The idea of social media needs to evolve into actual online socializing, where conversations can take any form.

- Public conversations 
- Private conversations 
- Semi-public conversations
    - where a designated group of people participate in a conversation in front of an audience
        - where the audience has a designated space for comments about the primary conversation
            - collapsible
            - Audience comments can be general or connected to specific comments in the primary conversation
            - Primary conversations can be initiated by anyone, who becomes first owner
            - First owner can invite anyone to the primary conversation, and assign their permissions
            - Audience comments are displayed in side column
                - both columns scroll proportionately, so that if a primary comment has many meta comments, the audience scrolls much faster than the primary.  Conversely, if a primary conversation has a low number of total comments, the primary scrolls faster than the audience
