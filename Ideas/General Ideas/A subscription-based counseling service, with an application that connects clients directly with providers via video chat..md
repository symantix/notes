A subscription-based counseling service, with an application that connects clients directly with providers via video chat.  Counselors are rated by the customers, broken down into a couple of vectors, including compassion, good fit, etc, and textual feedback.  60 minutes of counseling is included per month, and time starts and stops with the call connection, so a user can effectively split that across more than one counselor if they wish.  Our standard for who qualifies as a counselor should be somewhat broad, allowing for mostly user ratings and provider resumes to route out the unqualified.  The app is not recommended for mentally ill or suicidal users; it’s just a place to get something off your shoulders with someone who is not directly involved with your life (this is what I find to be most valuable about paid counseling).  Our app allows users to find counselors based on religion or lack thereof.  Users can be anonymized as needed.
